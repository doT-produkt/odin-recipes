Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwich

A peanut butter jelly sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jam of any kind

Instructions

  1. Put the slices of bread in the toaster
  2. once the bread is toasted spread the peanut butter on one slice and the jelly on the other
  3. put the two slices together with the peanut butter and the jelly facing eachother
