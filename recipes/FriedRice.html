Fried Rice

A quick rice dish that can have a variety of different ingredients

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • olive oil (any type of oil works but olive tends to work well and is healthy)
  • onions
  • some sort of protein

Instructions

  1. First you need to cook your rice if you have a rice cooker use that. If not use a pot with boiling water and cook using that
  2. once you have cooked your rice add olive oil to a pan and let the oil heat to a simmer
  3. Once your oil is simmering add your rice to the pan alongside other ingredients like onions
  4. If you want your onions to be more seared add them before your rice
  5. you can add your protein and maybe spice it up with some egg
  6. once your rice looks like its starting to fry add some soy sauce or other flavoring to the dish
  7. mix around the ingredients until it looks to be fully cooked
  8. and you are all done
